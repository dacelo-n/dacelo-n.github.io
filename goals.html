Goals

My overall goal is to be part of Machine Learning and Data Science in a broad sense. In a more specific way, I want to be a part of Natural Language Processing. I want to combine my love of Linguistics and programming in a manner that I can contribute to the field and build software, or help develop algorithms that will aid us in better understanding Linguistics. That can develop systems that can speak in life like giving company to the elderly, reaching those in need and to help minimize what is lost in translation when conducting business or research. I would also like to build a platform to teach people for free online and give opprtunities to those like me, not formally educated in Computer Science and trying to get their foot in the door. I'm a strong believer in giving back to an online community of people that are like minded, and contributing to free and open source software and learning.
